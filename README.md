# 铁框里的自己：家庭高压下跨性别女性的自我认同困境 / Self within Iron Frames: The Self-Identity Dilemma of Transgender Women Under High-Pressure Family

这是一篇使用论文为文体的形式小说，将使用论文这一文字形式，剖析在东亚家庭的期冀压力、父权制对于男孩子的期待、“多让人笑话”的好面子心理、以及对遵从上一代经验的求稳心态影响下，家庭这一社会最小单位对跨性别女性心理健康的负面影响。

This is a novel written in the style of an academic paper. It will use this academic paper format to analyze the negative impact of the family, the smallest unit of society, on the mental health of transgender women. This is influenced by the expectations and pressures of East Asian families, patriarchal expectations of boys, the desire to save face (ask for ridicule), and the mentality of seeking stability by following the experiences of the previous generation.
